Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I add timestamps to my YouTube videos?

Timestamps improve viewer experience by allowing them to jump to specific sections. They also help with SEO as YouTube can parse them to create video chapters.

What's the correct timestamp format?

The standard format is HH:MM:SS (hours:minutes:seconds). You can also use MM:SS for shorter videos.

Can I edit timestamps after generating them?

Yes! The output is editable - you can modify it directly in the output box before copying.

Is there a limit to how many timestamps I can add?

YouTube supports up to 100 chapters per video, but for practical purposes, 10-20 well-organized timestamps work best.

Do timestamps affect my video's SEO?

Yes! Properly formatted timestamps can improve your video's visibility in search results by providing more context to YouTube's algorithm.
